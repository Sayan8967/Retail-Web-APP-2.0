About Appliance Warehouse

Appliance Warehouse is a leading provider of high-quality kitchen, laundry, and house appliances, as well as replacement parts. Our mission is to provide our customers with the best possible products and services, while maintaining a commitment to sustainability and social responsibility.

Our Mission

Our mission is to exceed our customers' expectations by offering exceptional value and service, while providing a dynamic and challenging work environment for our employees.

History

Appliance Warehouse was founded by Mae Roth and has since grown into one of the largest appliance retailers in the country. Over the years, we have established a reputation for quality, affordability, and excellent customer service.

Our Goals

  • To continue to offer a wide range of high-quality products at competitive prices
  • To maintain a commitment to sustainability and social responsibility
  • To provide exceptional customer service and support
  • To foster a work environment that promotes employee growth and development

We are committed to achieving these goals and to earning the trust and loyalty of our customers.
